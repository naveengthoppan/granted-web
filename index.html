What Granted does

Everything you need to stay on top of your residency

📋

Stamp Tracker

Log every IRP stamp with its type, issue date, and expiry. Stamps are sorted by urgency — expiring first — with live valid, expiring, and expired status indicators.

⏳

Citizenship Eligibility

Know exactly how far you are from naturalisation. A real-time counter, 5-year progress bar, and estimated eligibility date — calculated from your stamps and travel history.

✈️

Travel Absence Log

Log trips outside Ireland so your citizenship clock stays accurate. Continuous residence warnings fire automatically when cumulative absences in your qualifying year approach the 70-day limit.

🔔

Renewal Reminders

Push notifications at 90, 60, and 30 days before your IRP card expires. An in-app banner activates automatically if notifications are turned off.

🏛️

Application Timeline

Track your citizenship application through all 14 official ISD stages — from submission to ceremony. Community benchmark timings will be populated as users contribute their own application data after launch.

📖

Stamp Info Library

Plain-English descriptions of all 12 Irish residence stamps — what each means, whether it counts toward citizenship, and how it fits your residency journey.

IRP Card Scanning

Scan your IRP card with your camera and Granted auto-fills stamp details. Text recognition runs on-device — your card image is never stored or transmitted. Structured parsing uses Anthropic's API (text only, no image).

From the founder

I've been in your shoes.

I moved to Ireland from India in August 2017 — a Masters student at the University of Limerick with no idea that Ireland would become home. Years later, I went through the citizenship process myself. Then again for my wife. Then for our children.

The application wasn't the hard part. Organising nearly a decade of documents was. And then came the moment that stuck with me: my wife had no utility bills in her name. For years, everything — the electricity, the broadband, the gas — was in mine. Nobody told us that would matter when the time came. There is no checklist handed to you at the immigration office on day one.

Granted is the app I wish had existed in 2017. Not just for people who are ready to apply — but for any non-EU immigrant, from the moment they land in Ireland. It might save you the cost of hiring an agent. It might save you a panicked night searching for a payslip from six years ago. But more than anything, it is built by someone who went through it, for the people going through it now.

Naveen George Thoppan iOS Developer · Founder, Granted
